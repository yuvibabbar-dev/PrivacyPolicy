Lane Flow – Privacy Policy

Effective: November 26, 2025

Summary

  • We do not require an account or collect personal data to use the app.
  • Session data (lanes, swimmers, timers, history) is stored locally on your device.
  • Purchases are processed by Apple via In‑App Purchase; receipts may be verified by RevenueCat.
  • No third-party ad SDKs are included; the app may show ads using Apple-approved frameworks only.
  • You control exports; CSV exports are generated locally and shared only when you choose.

Data We Handle

Local app data: Lanes, swimmer labels, timers, history, and preferences are stored on your device. Clearing app data or uninstalling removes this information.

Subscriptions: Apple processes payments. We use RevenueCat to manage entitlements and verify App Store receipts. RevenueCat may receive anonymized identifiers (e.g., app user ID) to maintain your subscription status.

Analytics/ads: We do not include third-party analytics SDKs. If ads are shown, they are limited to Apple-approved frameworks and do not use custom tracking.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Subscription status may be shared with Apple (IAP) and RevenueCat solely to manage purchases and access.

Exports and Sharing

Exports (e.g., CSV) are created locally and shared only when you initiate them through the system share sheet or file save dialogs.

Retention and Deletion

All session data is kept on your device. To remove it, delete app data or uninstall the app. Subscription records managed by Apple/RevenueCat are retained as required for purchase validation.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13.
